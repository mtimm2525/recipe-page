Chips and Salsa

Plated Chips and Salsa

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips of Choice
  • Sals of Choice
  • Two bowls or Bowl and Plate

Steps

  1. Pour salsa into one of the bowls or into the only bowl
  2. Do the same with the chips in the remaining bowl or pour them on the plate
  3. Place the bowls together or place the bowl on the plate with the chips and serve!
