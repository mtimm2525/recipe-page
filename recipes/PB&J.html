Sliced Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Spreading Utensil

Steps

  1. Place two pieces of bread on a plate
  2. Using your spreading utensil, scoop the desired amount of peanute butter onto one slice and spread it evenly
  3. Clean the utensil and do the same to the other slice with your jelly
  4. To finish the sandwich, put the two slices together with the PB and Jelly touching
